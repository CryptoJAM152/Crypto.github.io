About Us

CryptoJAM is established with the sole purpose to bridge the gap between the crypto space and the user base. We're building a secure and fast crypto payment environment around the world with the potential to re-define the payment experience into a single click payment. We provide, service to the vendor to accept payments in cryptocurrency or in their preferred currency.

CryptoJAM tends to provide a seamless experience for all users in the space for hustle free crypto payments. With us, micropayments have become much easier and faster.

CryptoJAM is a team of crypto enthusiasts backed by a promising mentor, with a dream to use cryptocurrency for daily use.

How does it Works?


"Helping people to reach a wide user-base with targeted campaigns designed to meet business"

  • 01 Step One

    Initially customer has to add their authorized wallets to start experiencing seamless crypto transactions with CryptoJAM.

  • 02 Step Two
